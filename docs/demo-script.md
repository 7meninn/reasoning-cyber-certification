# Demo Script

1. Start the app with `.\scripts\run_demo.ps1`.
2. Confirm `L-1001` is selected.
3. Click `Run Mock Demo`.
4. Open the Path tab and show Security+ foundation plus SC-200-oriented readiness.
5. Open Skill Gaps and call out incident response, KQL, and alert triage.
6. Open Study Plan and show the 4-week capacity-aware schedule.
7. Open Scenario Lab and review the suspicious sign-in artifacts.
8. Open Assessment and show the CONDITIONAL verdict plus remediation sprint.
9. Open Manager and show aggregate readiness and privacy note.
10. Open Agent Trace and show raw JSON, parsed outputs, citations, guardrails, fallback mode, and latency.
11. Click Reset Demo before rehearsing another run.
